Bucket Robotics

A high school robotics team with a mission to inspire students to pursue their interests in STEM education.

Who are we?

We are a robotics team based in Richmond, BC. Our team was founded in 2022 at R.C. Palmer Secondary School with the goal of making STEM education accessible to all students. We strive to create a welcoming environment for all students to learn about engineering and robotics. Regardless of your background, age, or experience, you are welcome to the club. We are passionate about robotics - we love to learn, build and create. We hope to inspire others with the same passion.

What we do

We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge - a robotics competition for students all around the world. We learn how to build, design, and program robots to compete in an engineering challenge against other competing teams.
